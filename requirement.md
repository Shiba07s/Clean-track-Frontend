Project Title (Sample)

“CleanTrack – A Smart Pollution Reporting & Reward System”

(You can change the name later.)


---

📌 Problem Statement

In many cities and towns, pollution such as garbage dumping, plastic waste, blocked drains, and dirty public places is increasing.

Current Problems:

❌ People ignore small pollution issues.

❌ Authorities get late or no information.

❌ No proper tracking system.

❌ Citizens are not motivated to report.

❌ No transparency about cleaning work.


Result:

Pollution spreads, affects health, and damages the environment.

👉 There is no simple digital system where people can report pollution and get feedback and rewards.


---

🎯 Objective of the Project

To create a website where:

Citizens can report pollution easily.

Authorities can verify and clean.

The system tracks all complaints.

Users get rewards for helping society.



---

💡 Proposed Solution

You will build a website where:

✅ Users can:

1. Take a photo of pollution.


2. Upload it with location.


3. Track their complaint.


4. Get rewards after verification.



✅ Admin can:

1. Verify complaints.


2. Visit location.


3. Fix the issue.


4. Upload “Before & After” photos.


5. Approve rewards.
